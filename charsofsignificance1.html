Jonathan Joestar: The Boy Wonder

Jonathan Joestar is the first protagonist of JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, often recognized for his unwavering sense of justice, chivalry, and compassion, he serves as the foundation of the Joestar legacy. His battle against Dio Brando sparks a generational conflict that defines the series.

-Significance

Jonathan, being the first JoJo is one of the most significant characters throughout all the parts. Although he doesn't have many appearances in other parts Jonathan's marriage to Erina Pendleton establishes the Joestar family lineage, a bloodline destined to clash with Dio Brando's evil for generations to come. He is the origin point of the “Joestar Curse,” and the everlasting conflict between his descendants and DIO's forces of darkness.
His most notable actions are the embodiment of nobility and sacrifice, ultimately sacrificing himself to defeat Dio, even at the cost of his own life, solidifying his heroic status.

Dio Brando: The Definitive Villain

Dio Brando is one of the most iconic villains in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure. His actions have an overpowering and lasting impact on the Joestar family, shaping the narrative across multiple generations.

-Significance

Dio's adoption into the Joestar family and his subsequent use of the Stone Mask to become a vampire sets in motion the central conflict that spans generations. His rivalry with Jonathan Joestar gives birth to the "Joestar Curse," a cycle of conflict between the Joestar bloodline and the forces of evil that Dio represents.
One of his most defining actions is his deliberate decision to use the Stone Mask and become a vampire symbolizes his rejection of his own humanity in pursuit of power.

Robert E.O. Speedwagon: The Steadfast Ally

Robert E.O. Speedwagon is a pivotal supporting character in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, often associated with his unwavering loyalty to the Joestar family and his instrumental role in supporting their fight against evil. He is introduced in Phantom Blood and his legacy extends throughout the series via the foundation that bears his name.

-Significance

Speedwagon's significance lies in his transition from a minor antagonist to an invaluable ally and his enduring support of the Joestar family. Initially a street thug encountered by Jonathan Joestar, Speedwagon quickly recognizes Jonathan's noble spirit and undergoes a dramatic change of heart. This transformation establishes him as a key ally and a testament to Jonathan's ability to inspire even the most hardened individuals.

-Speedwagon Foundation

After Jonathan's death, Speedwagon creates the Speedwagon Foundation, a powerful and influential organization dedicated to medical and paranormal research. This foundation plays a vital role in multiple parts of the series, providing resources, information, and logistical support to the protagonists.
His most defining actions are the establishment of the Speedwagon Foundation and the narrative exposition he provides to the readers.
